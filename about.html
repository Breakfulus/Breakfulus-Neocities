A bit of background

I'm a little ghost with some big dreams, specifically in making videogames. If the drawn asset didn't give it away, I come primarily from an artistic background. I've always loved drawing, I can't count how many sketchbooks I've filled, but its a lot! My mother was an artist, my sister was an artist, my cousin is an artist, it sorta just runs in the family. Eventually I moved on from traditional art, not entirely but mostly, and started making vector and pixel art, with a bit of digital art.

When I first learned about videogames, I was enamored, I have always had a love for figuring out how things work and videogames especially intrigued me, and were the reason I originally got into pixel art itself.
